## Introduction

As an experienced Electrical-Electronics Engineer, I specialize in the mechanical design of complex **electromechanical systems**. While electromagnetic design defines the performance, mechanical design ensures the feasibility, thermal stability, and manufacturability of the machine.

This repository features high-fidelity 3D CAD models of electric machines (PMSM, BLDC, SynRM) engineered in **SolidWorks**. My design process bridges the gap between electrical specifications and mechanical constraints, focusing on:
* **Thermal Management:** Housing and cooling fin designs optimized for heat dissipation.
* **Mechanical Integrity:** Proper bearing selection, shaft stress analysis, and interference fits.
* **Manufacturability (DfM):** Stator lamination stacking, winding slot insulation, and assembly tolerance checks.
